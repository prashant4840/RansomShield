# Pitch Script (Problem -> Solution -> Impact)

## Problem
Android ransomware encrypts files or hijacks lockscreens quickly. Signature-based AV often misses zero-day behavior and detects too late.

## Solution
RansomShield is an AI-powered early warning platform that detects ransomware **before encryption**, by modeling behavior anomalies in real time on-device.

## Why It Wins
- Behavior-based, not signature-based.
- Explainable AI alerts for trust and actionability.
- Lightweight and battery-aware.
- Offline-capable threat intelligence.
- Demo-friendly simulation mode with visual SOC dashboard.

## Impact
- Prevents data loss and device lockout.
- Useful for students, SMB teams, and BYOD enterprises.
- Extendable to cloud-synced mobile threat monitoring.
